For making Donuts:

2 Cup All purpose Flour (Maida) 
1/4 Cup Powdered sugar
3/4 Tsp Baking powder
1/2 Tsp Baking soda OR 1 Tsp ENO fruit salt
1/2 Cup Milk
2 Tsp Vinegar or Lemon juice
A pinch of salt
2-3 Tsp Water
2 Tbsp Butter or Ghee
Oil for frying



For Decoration:

1/2 Cup Dark/Milk chocolate
Some sprinkles
